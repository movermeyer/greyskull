Pluecast Sharing is caring

This is an experimental tracker!

Feel free to use for any torrent you like, but keep in mind that this tracker is mostly for experimental and research purposes. For an extensive compilation of public and open trackers that you can use and information about their reliability go to Trackon.

You can use the following address to add this tracker to your torrents:

http://pluecast.com/announce

Powered by the Atrack Tracker for Google App Engine.

Note: For privacy and efficiency reasons, atrack does not implement the scrape protocol and other non-essential BT features, so you wont see the number of seeders and leechers available in this tracker, this doesn't mean they aren't there, but atrack doesn't keep track of that information.
